# ☕ Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes historical transactional data from **Bright Coffee Shop** to provide actionable insights for the newly appointed CEO. The CEO’s mission is to **grow company revenue** and **improve product performance**.  
As a Junior Data Analyst, the goal is to extract insights, build dashboards, and prepare a presentation that supports strategic decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Top Products:** Coffee beverages generate the highest revenue, with specialty drinks outperforming standard options.  
- **Peak Hours:** Morning rush (7–9 AM) consistently shows the highest transaction volume.  
- **Store Performance:** Certain store locations outperform others, highlighting opportunities for expansion or targeted improvement.  
- **Product Trends:** Pastries and add-ons (like muffins and croissants) complement beverage sales, driving bundled purchases.  
- **Growth Momentum:** Revenue shows upward trends during weekends and festive seasons, indicating strong seasonal demand.  

---

## 🏪 Store Performance Highlights

- Urban stores consistently outperform suburban ones in both revenue and transaction volume.  
- Underperforming stores show lower average transaction values, suggesting weaker product mix or customer engagement.  
- Store-level insights guide **inventory planning** and **staffing optimization**.  

---

## ⏰ Time-of-Day Revenue Insights

- **Morning (7–9 AM):** Highest sales volume, dominated by coffee beverages.  
- **Afternoon (12–2 PM):** Moderate sales, driven by food items and light snacks.  
- **Evening (5–7 PM):** Lower sales, potential opportunity for promotions or loyalty programs.  

---

## 🧁 Product Trends

- **Best-Selling Categories:** Coffee and pastries.  
- **Top Product Types:** Lattes, cappuccinos, and muffins.  
- **Underperforming Items:** Certain niche drinks and specialty snacks with consistently low demand.  
- Recommendation: Promote weak products through bundling or rebranding.  

---

## 📈 Growth Momentum

- Seasonal spikes during holidays and weekends.  
- Consistent growth in specialty beverages indicates evolving customer preferences.  
- Opportunities exist to expand product lines aligned with customer demand trends.  

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Launch **marketing campaigns** during slow time slots (afternoons/evenings).  
- **Stock more of best-selling items** to meet demand.  
- **Promote underperforming products** via discounts or bundles.  
- Align **staffing schedules** with peak hours to improve efficiency.  
- Explore **expansion opportunities** in high-performing store locations.  
- Implement **loyalty programs** to boost repeat purchases.
